How Thropic Games Works

Our platform connects nonprofits, brands, and players through engaging games that raise funds and awareness for important causes.

The Process

1. Nonprofits and Brands Partner

Nonprofits and brands come together to create a game that aligns with their missions and values. Brands provide funding and prizes, while nonprofits contribute their cause and community.

  • For Nonprofits: Zero upfront costs, access to new donors, increased awareness
  • For Brands: Authentic engagement, purpose-driven marketing, positive brand association

2. Game Creation and Launch

Our team works with partners to design and develop an engaging game that incorporates the nonprofit's mission and the brand's identity. Once ready, the game is launched on our platform.

  • Game Types: Casual Games, Competitions, Challenges

3. Players Join and Participate

Players discover games through our platform, social media, or partner promotions. They make donations to enter games and compete for prizes while supporting causes they care about.

  • Player Experience: Browse Games, Make a Donation, Play and Compete

4. Impact and Results

Funds raised through gameplay are distributed to the nonprofit partners. All stakeholders receive detailed reports on engagement, impact, and outcomes.

  • Nonprofits: Receive funds and new supporters for their cause
  • Brands: Gain positive exposure and meaningful engagement
  • Players: Enjoy games, win prizes, and make a difference

Our Transparent Donation Model

1. Player Donations

Players make donations to enter games, with minimum amounts set by the nonprofit.

2. Fee Structure

Thropic Games takes a small platform fee (10%) to maintain and improve the platform.

3. Nonprofit Distribution

90% of all donations go directly to the nonprofit partner(s) associated with the game.

4. Transparent Reporting

All stakeholders receive detailed reports on donations, fees, and distributions.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do nonprofits get started?

Nonprofits can sign up on our platform, complete a simple verification process, and then either create their own game or get matched with a brand partner.

How are donations processed?

Donations are processed securely through our platform using major payment methods. Funds are held in escrow until the campaign ends, then distributed to nonprofits.

Are donations tax-deductible?

Yes, donations made through our platform to registered 501(c)(3) organizations are tax-deductible to the extent allowed by law. Players receive receipts for their donations.

How are winners selected?

Winners are determined based on the specific rules of each game, which may include highest scores, fastest times, or random drawings. All selection processes are transparent.

Can international nonprofits participate?

Yes, we work with registered charitable organizations worldwide, though specific requirements may vary by country. Contact us for details about your region.

How long do campaigns typically run?

Campaign lengths vary based on goals and game types, but most run for 2-4 weeks. Longer campaigns are possible for special events or ongoing initiatives.
