Frequently Asked Questions

How do I enter a game?

Simply browse our available games, select one you're interested in, and make the required donation to enter. You'll receive immediate access to play.

Where does my donation go?

Your donation goes directly to the nonprofit organization associated with the game, minus a small platform fee that helps us maintain and improve Thropic Games.

How are winners selected?

Winners are typically determined by gameplay performance, such as highest score or fastest completion time. Each game has its own specific rules and criteria.

Can I play on my mobile device?

Yes! All of our games are designed to work seamlessly on desktop and mobile devices, so you can play anytime, anywhere.
