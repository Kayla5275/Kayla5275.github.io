How Thropic Games Works

Nonprofit Creates a Game

Nonprofits create engaging games that align with their mission and values.

Sets Low Entry Fee

A small entry fee is set for players to join the game and support the cause.

Players Deposit, Play & Share

Players join games, make donations, and share with friends to increase impact.

Donations + Prizes = Social Impact

Funds are raised through gameplay and distributed to nonprofits with full transparency.
