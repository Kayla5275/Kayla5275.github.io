Frequently Asked Questions

How much does it cost to get started?

There are no upfront costs for nonprofits. We operate on a revenue-sharing model, taking a small percentage of donations raised through our platform.

How long does it take to create a game?

Typically, we can have a custom game ready to launch within 2-4 weeks, depending on complexity and customization requirements.

How do we receive the funds raised?

Funds are distributed directly to your organization's bank account on a monthly basis, with detailed reporting on all transactions.

Can we customize the game to match our brand?

We work closely with your team to ensure the game reflects your organization's mission, values, and visual identity.
